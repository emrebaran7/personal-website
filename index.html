About Me

My creative side has always driven me forward in my life. I am a self-taught musician who loves electronic music but is not committed to a particular genre (Art is authenticity!). I love talking about political theory, aesthetics and bouncing ideas off my friends. I pride my self on being able to think abstractly and then zoom into practical applications. This has enabled my success in strategy consulting in the last 6 years and also informs my approach to coding, which is collaborative, methodical, logical and communicative.

As a software engineer, I enjoy using Ruby, JavaScript, React/Redux, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, CSS, and HTML. I am relentlessly curious and enjoy being an information sponge in all aspects of my life.
